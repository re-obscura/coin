How do we grade coins in Leesburg, VA?

In Leesburg, VA, coin grading is essentially a sociocultural phenomenon in which experts and collectors share knowledge, perpetuating the value of these numismatic gems.

1

Historical Context

Understanding origin, minting date, and historical era. Critical for establishing value.

2

Physical Inspection

Carried out with utmost care. Gentle cleaning with distilled water only if required.

3

Lighting & Magnification

Using LED ring lights and 5x-10x loupes to detect possible flaws and examine small details.

4

Surface Condition

Checking for wear, scratches, and original shine. Comparing to certified samples.

5

Mintage Quality

Clear, crisp lines usually receive higher grades, indicating high quality workmanship.

6

Authentication

Ruling out counterfeiting via comparison, spectral analysis, or databases. Assigning Sheldon scale grade (1-70).
