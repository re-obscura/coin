How do we grade coins in Alexandria, VA?

Coin appraisal in Alexandria, VA is a process that brings together collectors and dealers. The primary goal is to determine the condition and assign a grade from 1 to 70 on the Sheldon scale.

1

History & Context

Detailed study of origin, minting process, and historical significance to accurately assess unique features.

2

Physical Grading

Gentle cleaning with distilled water only. Harsh chemicals are discouraged as they reduce value.

3

Lighting & Magnification

Using LED ring lights and 5x-10x loupes to reveal hidden details, marks, and minor imperfections.

4

Surface Preservation

Examining for wear, scratches, and original luster. Tiny details significantly affect the final grade.

5

Quality Analysis

Testing how well the design was conveyed during minting. distinct images get higher grades.

6

Authentication

Checking authenticity via comparison, spectral analysis, or counterfeit databases.
