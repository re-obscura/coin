How do we grade coins in Arlington, VA?

Coin grading is a thorough process. The purpose is to determine the condition and assign a numerical value from 1 to 70 on the Sheldon Scale.

1

History & Context

Thorough research to understand provenance, minting process, and historical significance.

2

Physical Evaluation

Gentle cleaning with distilled water only. Aggressive chemicals should never be used.

3

Lighting & Magnification

Using LED ring lights and 5x-10x loupes to reveal fine details like mint marks.

4

Surface Preservation

Inspecting for wear, scratches, and original luster. Minute details significantly affect the grade.

5

Mint Quality

Assessing how well the design details were conveyed. Crisp strokes receive higher grades.

6

Authentication

Verifying authenticity via spectral analysis or databases to identify fakes.
