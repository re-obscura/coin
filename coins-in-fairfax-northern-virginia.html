Coin grading in Fairfax, Northern Virginia

Coin grading in Fairfax, Northern Virginia is a meticulous process that combines art and science. It is a combination of historical erudition, accurate scientific research, and an understanding of fine craftsmanship.

1

Initial Inspection

Scrutinizing coins for visible anomalies or distinctive features to determine the coin’s era of origin.

2

Physical Condition

Checking for corrosion or wear using a standardized scale from “poor” to “like new”.

3

Patina Analysis

Assessing authenticity and age. A well-preserved patina often indicates a long and intact history.

4

Advanced Tech

Using X-ray fluorescence (XRF) to determine metal composition without damage and detect fakes.
