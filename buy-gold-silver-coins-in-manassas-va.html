How do we grade gold and silver in Manassas, VA?

Gold and silver valuation is a complex process involving analyzing purity, weight, condition, and rarity. Understanding this process is essential for informed decisions.

1

Purity Analysis

Measuring fineness (karats for gold, thousandths for silver). Using fire assay or XRF for accurate, non-destructive testing.

2

Considering Weight

Weighing in troy ounces. Accuracy is critical, as even small differences affect value significantly.

3

Condition Grading

Assessing physical condition and wear. Using Sheldon scale (1-70) for coins. Jewelry graded visually/microscopically.

4

Rarity & History

Limited quantities or historical significance can increase value beyond metal content.

5

Certification

Trusted agencies (PCGS, NGC) increase reliability. Encapsulation provides authoritative confirmation of authenticity.

6

Comprehensive Process

Combining scientific expertise with historical and market aspects for a complete valuation.
