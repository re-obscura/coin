How do we grade gold coins in Fairfax, VA?

Gold coin grading is a meticulous process combining art and science. It involves assessing physical and aesthetic characteristics to determine overall condition and value.

1

Surface Examination

Using magnification to inspect for wear, scratches, or dents. Ideally, coins retain crisp detail in lettering and images.

2

Sheldon Scale

Grading on a scale of 1 to 70. 70 represents a flawless specimen. Lower grades show significant wear.

3

Overall Appeal

Considering luster, color, and tone. Patina can sometimes add value depending on market trends.

4

Authenticity

Using advanced techniques like chemical analysis and examining mint marks to ensure the coin is not counterfeit.

5

Professional Services

Offering encapsulation, official certificates, and assurance from experienced professionals.

6

Value Establishment

This process is critical for establishing a resale value in the marketplace.
