How do we grade jewelry?

When you bring a piece of jewelry to us, a careful and detailed process begins. This process includes several steps, each of which has its own significance.

1

Inspection & Authenticity

We determine the authenticity of metals (gold, silver, platinum) and gemstones, checking clarity, weight, and cut.

2

Condition Assessment

We assess signs of wear. Note: Antique jewelry may have high value despite wear due to historical uniqueness.

3

Origin & History

Researching the pedigree. A piece with a rich history passing through generations can have significant added value.

4

Final Estimate

Based on current market prices for precious metals/stones and demand. Honest and accurate appraisal is key.
