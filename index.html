Paper Currency

Our expertise extends to collectible currency, including:

  • Vintage U.S. Paper Money: Gold certificates, silver certificates (blue and yellow seal), National Bank Notes.

Stamps

We buy stamp collections with at least $500 total face value.

  • Collections of rarities and limited editions.
  • New Albums should include SHEETS, BOOKS, or Rolls of usable STAMPS.
  • Non-franked and uncancelled stamps.
  • Note: We don’t purchase first day of release or first edition stamps.
