    Your Personalized Consultation: By Appointment for Optimal Service

    To provide each client with the detailed attention they deserve, all evaluations and transactions at Fairfax Coin and Bullion Exchange are conducted by appointment only.

    To make the most of your consultation:

    • If possible, please sort loose, circulated coins by denomination. Items already in protective holders (flips, albums, slabs) should remain undisturbed.
    • Crucially, do not clean your coins. Cleaning can significantly reduce their numismatic value. We assess items in their current state.
    • For extensive collections or numerous jewelry pieces, please provide an overview when scheduling so we can allocate appropriate time for a thorough review.
