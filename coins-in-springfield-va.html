How do we grade coins in Springfield, VA?

We specialize in the buying and selling of coins in Springfield, VA, providing expert appraisals based on historical background and meticulous examination.

1

Historical Background

Knowing the coin’s origin, minting date, and era are key factors. Thorough research uncovers production details.

2

Delicate Cleaning

Using only distilled water and a gentle cloth. Harsh chemicals are avoided to prevent harm and value loss.

3

Lighting & Magnification

LED ring lights and 5x-10x loupes enable detailed scrutiny of minute features and potential imperfections.

4

Surface Condition

Crucial examination for wear and scratches. Original shine and handling marks greatly impact appraisal.

5

Minting Quality

Coins with sharp, well-defined lines reflect superior skill and typically receive superior grades.

6

Authentication

Assessing against authenticated specimens via spectral analysis and databases. Assigning Sheldon scale grade (1-70).
