How do we grade coins in Herndon, VA?

Coin grading in Herndon, VA is a meticulous process that combines both art and science to determine the quality and condition of a coin.

1

Close Inspection

Looking for signs of wear or damage, such as scratches, dents, or discoloration.

2

Industry Standards

Comparing to NGC and PCGS standards. Using the universal scale from poor (P-1) to perfect (MS-70).

3

Detailed Attributes

Considering luster, strike, color, and appeal, along with historical background and significance in a series.

4

Provenance

Understanding origin. Coins with documented history, especially associated with significant events, have higher value.

5

Advanced Technology

Using digital microscopes for high-resolution imaging to determine smallest details and ensure repeatable grading.

6

Collaborative Effort

Combining expertise, technology, and community spirit to provide accurate grading.
